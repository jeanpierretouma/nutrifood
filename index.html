How it works

Healthy meals in 3 simple steps

01

Choose what you like and dislike

Spend no more time wondering what to eat! A customized weekly meal plan will be created for you by the Nutrifood app. This ensures you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need, no matter what lifestyle you lead!

02

Approve your weekly meal plan

Each week, approve the meal plan that Nutrifood generates for you. You can replace whole meals, change ingredients, or create your own recipes.

03

Receive meals at convenient times

Your meals will be prepared by the best chefs in town every day, and we will deliver them to your door whenever it's convenient for you. You can adjust your delivery schedule and address each day!
